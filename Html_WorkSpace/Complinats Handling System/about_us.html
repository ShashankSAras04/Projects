About CHS

Empowering citizens through digital complaint resolution

What is CHS?

The Complaint Handling System (CHS) is a platform built to bridge the communication gap between citizens and law enforcement. With easy registration, tracking, and management of complaints, we aim to build transparency and trust in public services.

CHS is designed to be fast, efficient, and reliable — eliminating paperwork and enabling real-time progress tracking.

Our Vision

To create a society where every citizen’s voice is heard, their concerns addressed transparently, and law enforcement is empowered with technology to serve better and faster.

Our Mission

To digitize the entire complaint management lifecycle — from registration to resolution — and make the process accessible, efficient, and trustworthy for every individual regardless of location or background.
